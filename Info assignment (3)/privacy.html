PRIVACY POLICY

In-Game Settings:

Open the Free Fire game on your mobile device. Look for a "Settings" or "Options" menu. Within the settings, there might be a section called "Privacy" or "Legal." Check for links to the privacy policy or terms of service.

Official Website:

Visit the official Garena Free Fire website using a web browser. Navigate to the "Privacy Policy" or "Terms of Service" section. This is usually found in the website's footer or under a dedicated legal section.

Mobile App Store:

On the app store (Google Play Store for Android or Apple App Store for iOS), you may find a link to the privacy policy on the Free Fire app page.

Contact Support:

If you have trouble finding the privacy policy, you can contact Free Fire support for assistance. There might be a customer support section within the game or on the official website where you can submit queries.
